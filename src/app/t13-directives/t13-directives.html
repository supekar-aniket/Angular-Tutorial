Directives

  • Directives are classes that add additional behavior to elements in your Angular applications.
  • A feature that help you to provide more power to DOM element.

Types

  1. Component Directives
    • The most common type of Direective.
    • Used in component template file.
  2. Structural Directives
    • Change the structure of the DOM by adding removing or manipulating element.
  3. Attribute Directives
    • Modify the appearence or behaviour of an existing element.
